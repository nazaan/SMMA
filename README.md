# SMMA
AI-powered automation platform for social media marketing

Purpose:
Automatically plan, generate, post, and (optionally) engage with your audience on social media for your products, saving time and boosting reach.
Key Features (full vision):

Post automation: Create social media posts for multiple platforms (Twitter/X, Instagram, TikTok, etc.) based on your product, audience, and campaign goals.

AI content generation: Generate captions, hashtags, and image/video suggestions.

Scheduling & posting: Automatically post on selected dates/times.

Engagement automation (optional): Like/reply to comments or messages intelligently (careful with social platform rules).

Analytics & dashboard: Track likes, shares, comments, reach, engagement rate.

Multi-account support: Manage multiple social media accounts from one platform.

Target Users:

Product owners / small businesses who want social media presence without spending hours daily.

Solo marketers who want automation + AI content.

MVP Goal

Allow a user to create AI-generated social media posts for their products, schedule them, and automatically post to one social media platform, with optional basic engagement tracking.
